Brycki

Brycki is a company specializing in the sale of window and door systems and provides comprehensive assembly and construction services. My task was to create a UI of simple page informing about the range of products and services. As for the colours, I suggested yellow and deep dark blue. Dark blue is a serious masculine colour representing knowledge, power, and integrity. It is also often associated with conservatism and responsibility. On the other hand, yellow represents energy and confidence. Together, they create a high-contrast combination that is often found in construction companies. When it comes to choosing typography, I chose to use a solid, reliable Gotham family font.
