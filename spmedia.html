Advertising agency

SPMEDIA

SP Media is an advertising agency focusing on activities in the area of the Internet. The main values that the brand wants to communicate are creativity, positive energy, excitement, taking action, development, and imagination.
My task was to create a new UX and graphic design for the website. During the creation of mock-ups and the design system, I wanted to combine the key brand values with the modern nature of the services provided by the company. That is why I proposed a graphic layout with modern navigation and the use of a smooth scroll. The website contains interactive elements, a video player and animated illustrations.
When it comes to colours, I proposed a combination of orange, violet and indigo. The colour orange radiates warmth, combining the physical energy and stimulation of red with the cheerfulness of yellow. It is often perceived as a colour of adventure and optimism. On the other hand, violet relates to imagination and creativity.
In this project, I used geometric sans serif typeface Poppins.
