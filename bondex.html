My main task was to create UX mockups and graphic layouts. The task for UX/UI side was to create a comprehensive website presenting new products, including wireframes and layouts. The main directions of communication defined in cooperation with the client are: color presentation, presentation of technical properties and the inspirational part.
The product is premium class, mainly aimed at young people showing an interest in interior design to some extent. The factor distinguishing the brand was the value of fun and freedom.
The choice of light green was due to the use of this color in packaging and marketing materials. It referred to the value of fun in communication. As a complementary color, I proposed a dark sea color. A more sophisticated version of turquoise, teal signifies trustworthiness and reliability, but also newest trend in interior design.
When it comes to typography, the choice of font for headings is related to that used in brand identification. As for the paragraph, I decided to choose a simple, clean and modern sans-serif font.
