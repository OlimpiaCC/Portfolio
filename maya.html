MAYA

Maya is a hair salon. My task was to create a simple, modern, clean website referring to the character of the brand. I was responsible for both design and front-end.
Maya is a salon aimed mainly at young women, specializing in hair styling. A large part of her clients are young women looking for a specialist to create a unique wedding styling. That is why Maya wants to communicate as a feminine, romantic brand with a strong emphasis on simplicity and modernity.
I offered the company a feminine, slightly dreamy colour scheme with modern, contrasting accents of black and grey.
Using an external library, I used a modern scrolling and navigation system.
For the headlines, I used the font Giaza, Didot typeface, with a variety of Art Deco Glyphs. In paragraphs, I used Brandon Grotesque, distinctive elegant geometrical font.

SCOPE: UI, Front-end
